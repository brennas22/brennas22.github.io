Fountaine Lab for Innovative Prosthetics

The Fountaine Lab for Innovative Prosthetics is a lab at Northeastern University funded through a grant from Ford. We work to reduce the cost of prosthetics using 3D printing, specifically focusing on users whose needs are not met through a typical prosthetic. I contribute to the design of these prosthetics through hand drawing and CAD modeling. Pictured is the preliminary design and first successful test print of an adjustable ankle for a high heeled shoe. It was modeled in Google SketchUp and printed on a Markforged printer. The project is still in progress.
