Here's the basics: my name is Brenna, my pronouns are she/her, and I'm a designer!

Why

I have experience in strategy and design research, as well as pixel pushing and front end development (I built this website!).

My expertise in accessibility intersects with my love for design, as I am passionate about ensuring that all people have equal access to services and products. This website complies with all ADA 508 standards for contrast and is keyboard & screenreader navigable.

I graduated magna cum laude from Northeastern University in Boston, MA with a degree in UX Design and Computer Science. I graduated with University Honors Program Distinction, Honors in the College of Arts, Media, and Design, and as a member of Northeastern's Huntington 100.
