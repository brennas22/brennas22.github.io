The Problem:

Inconvenience related to husky cards: long waits to get into dorms, long waits at dining halls, losing your husky card

Our Solution: Wearable Husky Cards

Types of RFID Systems:

3 types of RFID wristbands available: fabric with a hard plastic chip inside, vinyl wristbands with an RFID microchip, a rubber wristband with an RFID chip

3 types of RFID systems:

3 types of RFID wristbands available: Passive- No battery, utilizes radio energy from the reader (Current Husky Cards use passive RFID systems), Active- No reader necessary, contains an onboard battery, Battery-Assisted- Battery only powers in presence of a reader

Functionality:

  1. Sender sends radio singal
  2. Tag antenna receives signal and powers chip
  3. Chip modifies signal
  4. Antenna sends radio signal back
  5. Receiver receives modified signal and extracts information from tag
