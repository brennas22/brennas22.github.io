Hello!

Who am I?

My name is Brenna Sorkin and I'm a full stack designer (a not-frequently-used term which I believe should have its day in the sun). I spend my time focusing on how people interact with the world, and I want to make those experiences both fruitful and delightful. I'm most passionate about social determinants of health and I'm particularly interested in food systems. I make accessibility a prority in everything I design, inculding this website. If you so desire you can read more about me.
