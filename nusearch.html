PlanEDU

These mockups are of a co-op search and class planning website that is an in progress self-directed project. I often find myself looking for information on students who've co-op'd at companies I'm interest in to identify what they studied and what courses they took. Additionally, I want to use that information to plan my classes for the next foreseable number of semesters. Northeastern has very customizable major requirements, which makes knowing which classes one needs to take a complicated process.
