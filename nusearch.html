Co-op Search and Class Planning

These mockups of a co-op search and class planning website are part of a project that I did on my own during the summer of 2016. I often found myself looking for information on students who've co-op'd at companies I'm interest in to identify what they studied and what courses they took. Additionally, I want to use that information to plan my classes for the next foreseeable number of semesters. Northeastern has very customizable major requirements, which makes knowing whhich classes one needs to take a complicated process.
