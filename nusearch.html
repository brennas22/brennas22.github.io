Co-op Search and Class Planning

These mockups of a co-op search and class planning website are part of a project that I did on my own over this summer. I often find myself wanting to look at information on students who've co-oped at interesting or relevant places in the past in order to look at what they studied and what courses they took. Additionally, I want to use that information to plan my classes for the next foreseeable number of semesters. Northeastern has very customizable major requirements, which makes know what one needs to take complicated.
